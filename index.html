The Legend of AD JEWELS

For nearly half a century, we have preserved the sacred art of Tamil Nadu's royal temple craftsmanship, translating ancient history into wearable sculpture.

🏺

Imperial Heritage

Established in 1980, we dedicated our sanctuary to reviving the lost repoussé gold work, thick collar-set kemp necklaces, and iconic temple jewelry of the Chola, Pandya, and Nayak dynasties.

✨

Royal Goldsmiths

Our masterpieces are hand-chiseled exclusively by fourth-generation hereditary goldsmiths. Using classical wax-casting, wirework filigree, and hand-beating, every curve encapsulates ancestral devotion.

📜

Sovereign Guarantee

Every creation features 100% BIS Hallmarked 22 Karat gold, accompanied by certified ethical conflict-free diamonds, and natural unheated rubies, emeralds, and pearls of absolute sovereign purity.

👑

Bespoke Sanctuary

Through our digital vault and bespoke inquiry systems, we invite patrons to co-design their ornaments, selecting custom weights, gemstones, and historic engravings to match their imperial story.

The Heritage Collections

Four distinct epochs of South Indian royal craftsmanship, curated for the modern connoisseur.

❂
9th Century Chola Era

The Chola Legacy

Bold repoussé gold work, thick collar-set kemp necklaces, and iconic heavy temple Oddiyanams designed for queens.

🏛️
7th Century Pallava Dynasty

Pallava Monoliths

Exquisite gold wire filigree, relief sculptures of celestial peacocks, and monolithic carved gemstones directly inspired by the stone Shore Temples of Mahabalipuram.

🍃
Sangam Period Literature

Sangam Splendor

Delicate leaf scrollwork, floral gold armlets (Vanki), and minimal, poet-praised geometric gemstones.

👑
Thanjavur Maratha Court

Royal Durbar

Exquisite filigree patterns, mango structures studded with flawless uncut diamonds, and rich enamel drops.
